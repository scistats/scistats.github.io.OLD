连续型随机变量$X,Y$ 具有联合密度函数\[ f\left( x,y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} \frac{1}{96}xy, & 0< x<4,1< y< 5\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] 确定$U=XY^{2},V=X^{2}Y$ 的联合密度函数.
求解变量代换方程$u=xy^{2},$ $v=x^{2}y$, 得到$x=v^{2/3}u^{-1/3},$ $y=u^{2/3}v^{-1/3}.$ 由\[ 0< v^{2/3}u^{-1/3}< 4,1< u^{2/3}v^{-1/3}< 5 \] 得到\[ v^{2}< 64u,v< u^{2}< 125v. \] Jacobian行列式为\[ J=\left\vert \begin{array} [c]{cc} -\frac{1}{3}v^{2/3}u^{-4/3} & \frac{2}{3}v^{-1/3}u^{-1/3}\\ \frac{2}{3}v^{-1/3}u^{-1/3} & -\frac{1}{3}v^{-4/3}u^{2/3} \end{array} \right\vert =-\frac{1}{3}v^{-2/3}u^{-2/3} \] 因此$U,V$ 的联合密度函数为\[ g\left( u,v\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} \frac{1}{288}u^{-1/3}v^{-1/3}, & v^{2}< 64u,v< u^{2}< 125v\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \]
随机变量$X$与$Y$ 相互独立, 其密度函数分别为\[ f_{1}\left( x\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} 2e^{-2x}, & x\geqslant0\\ 0, & x<0 \end{array} \right. ,f_{2}\left( y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} 3e^{-3y}, & y\geqslant0\\ 0, & y<0 \end{array} \right. \] 确定$U=X+Y$的密度函数.
由卷积公式, $u\geqslant0$ 时(注意$f_{1},f_{2}$的定义域) \begin{align*} f_{U}\left( u\right) & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f_{1}\left( x\right) f_{2}\left( u-x\right) dx\\ & =\int_{0}^{u}2e^{-2x}3e^{-3\left( u-x\right) }dx\\ & =6e^{-3u}\int_{0}^{u}e^{x}dx=6e^{-3u}\left( e^{u}-1\right) \end{align*} $u<0$时$f_{U}\left( u\right) =0.$
随机变量$X$与$Y$ 具有联合分布密度\[ f\left( x,y\right) =\frac{1}{2\pi}\exp\left( -\frac{x^{2}+y^{2}}{2}\right) ,x,y\in\mathbb{R} \] 试求$Z=\sqrt{X^{2}+Y^{2}}$ 的分布密度.
由于$Z$非负, $z\leqslant0$ 时分布$F_{Z}\left( z\right) =0.$ $z>0$时\begin{align*} F_{Z}\left( z\right) & =\int\int_{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}\leqslant z}f\left( x,y\right) dxdy\\ & =\frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{0}^{z}\exp\left( -\frac{r^{2}} {2}\right) rdrd\theta\\ & =1-e^{-\frac{z^{2}}{2}} \end{align*} 这里运用了直角坐标到极坐标的变量代换, 变换面基元关系为$dxdy=rdrdd\theta $. 因此$Z$的密度为\[ f_{Z}\left( z\right) =\left( F_{Z}\left( z\right) \right) ^{\prime }=\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} ze^{-\frac{z^{2}}{2}}, & z>0\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \]
两个人约好在某日下午$2$ 点至$3$点之间见面, 并同意让对方等待的时间不超过$0.25$ 小时. 确定两人能碰面的概率.
用$X$与$Y$ 分别表示两人到达时距离$2$ 点这一时刻的时间长度, 由题$X$与$Y$相互独立, 并且服从$\left[ 0,1\right] $ 上的均匀分布, 因此$X$ 与$Y$的联合密度为$,$ \[ f\left( x,y\right) =f_{X}\left( x\right) f_{Y}\left( y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} 1, & x,y\in\left[ 0,1\right] \\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] 所求概率\[ P\left( \left\vert X-Y\right\vert \leqslant0.25\right) =\int\int_{\left\vert x-y\right\vert \leqslant0.25}f\left( x,y\right) dxdy=7/16. \] (根据积分的几何意义, 用图示法可直接得到结果)
（随机变量的线性变换）假设随机变量$\mathbf{X} =\left( X_{1},...,X_{n}\right) $ 的联合密度函数为$f\left( x_{1},...,x_{n}\right) .$ 令随机变量$\mathbf{Y} =\left( Y_{1},...,Y_{n}\right) =\mathbf{AX}$, 其中$\mathbf{A} $是非奇异的$n\times n$矩阵. 试证明$\mathbf{Y}$ 的密度函数为\[ g\left( \mathbf{y}\right) =\frac{f\left( \mathbf{A}^{-1}\mathbf{y}\right) }{\left\vert \det\mathbf{A}\right\vert },\forall\mathbf{y}\in\mathbb{R}^{n} \]
由于$\mathbf{A}$是非奇异, 因此$\mathbf{Y}=\mathbf{AX}$ 确定一个一一变换, $\mathbf{X}=\mathbf{A}^{-1}\mathbf{Y}$. $\mathbf{Y}$ 关于$\mathbf{X}$ 的Jacobian行列式正是$\det\mathbf{A}$, 并注意\[ \det\mathbf{A}^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det\mathbf{A}} \] 由密度变换公式即得到所要证明的结论.
随机变量$X$与$Y$ 具有联合分布密度\[ f\left( x,y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} 12y^{2}, & 0\leqslant y\leqslant x\leqslant1\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] 试求出$E\left( XY\right) $
由期望计算公式, \begin{align*} E\left( XY\right) & =\int_{0}^{1}\left( \int_{0}^{x}xy12y^{2}dy\right) dx\\ & =3\int_{0}^{1}x\left( \int_{0}^{x}4y^{3}dy\right) dx\\ & =3\int_{0}^{1}x^{5}dx=\frac{1}{2}. \end{align*}
假设$X_{1},...,X_{n}$ 相互独立,都服从$\left[ 0,1\right] $上的均匀分布. 试求出下列随机变量的期望 \[ Y_{1}=\min\left\{ X_{1},X_{2},...,X_{n}\right\} ,Y_{n}=\max\left\{ X_{1},X_{2},...,X_{n}\right\} \]
$X_{k}$的分布函数为\[ F\left( x\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} 0, & x\leqslant0,\\ x, & 0< x\leqslant1,\\ 1, & x>1; \end{array} \right. \] 根据相关例题可知$Y_{n} $的分布函数为$F_{Y_{n}}\left( x\right) =F\left( x\right) ^{n}$, 密度为\[ f_{Y_{n}}\left( x\right) =\left( F_{Y_{n}}\left( x\right) \right) ^{\prime}=\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} nx^{n-1}, & 0\leqslant x\leqslant1\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] 因此\[ E\left( Y_{n}\right) =\int_{0}^{1}xf_{Y_{n}}\left( x\right) dx=\frac {n}{n+1}. \] $Y_{1}$的分布函数为$F_{Y_{1}}\left( x\right) =1-\left( 1-F\left( x\right) \right) ^{n}$, 密度为\[ f_{Y_{1}}\left( x\right) =\left( F_{Y_{1}}\left( x\right) \right) ^{\prime}=\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} n\left( 1-x\right) ^{n-1}, & 0\leqslant x\leqslant1\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] 因此\begin{align*} E\left( Y_{1}\right) & =\int_{0}^{1}xf_{Y_{1}}\left( x\right) dx\\ & =\int_{0}^{1}n\left( 1-x\right) ^{n-1}dx-\int_{0}^{1}n\left( 1-x\right) ^{n}dx\\ & =\frac{1}{n+1}. \end{align*}
从$\left[ 0,1\right] $ 以均匀分布取出一点$X_{1} $, 观测到$X_{1}=x_{1}\in\left( 0,1\right) $ 后又在$\left[ x_{1},1\right] $ 以均匀分布取出一点$X_{2} ,$ 观测到$X_{2}=x_{2}\in\left( x_{1},1\right) $后又在$\left[ x_{2},1\right] $ 以均匀分布取出一点$X_{3} ,...$这样一直继续下去, 直到观测到$X_{n-1}=x_{n-1}\in\left( x_{n-2},1\right) $后又在$\left[ x_{n-1},1\right] $ 以均匀分布取出一点$X_{n} $. 试求出$E\left( X_{n}\right) .$ (提示：首先考虑$n=2$ 的情形)
首先容易看出, $EX_{1}=\int_{0} ^{1}xdx=\frac{1}{2}.$ 由题意, 观测到$X_{n-1}=x^{\prime}$的条件下, $X_{n}$的密度为\[ f_{X_{n}}\left( x|x^{\prime}\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} \frac{1}{1-x^{\prime}}, & x^{\prime}\leqslant x\leqslant1\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] 如果用$f_{X_{n-1}}\left( x^{\prime}\right) $ 表示$X_{n-1}$的密度, 那么$X_{n} $与$X_{n-1}$ 的联合分布应该是\[ f\left( x,x^{\prime}\right) =f_{X_{n}}\left( x|x^{\prime}\right) f_{X_{n-1}}\left( x^{\prime}\right) \] 因此关于$X_{n}$ 的边际密度为\[ f_{X_{n}}\left( x\right) =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f\left( x,x^{\prime }\right) dx^{\prime}=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f_{X_{n}}\left( x|x^{\prime }\right) f_{X_{n-1}}\left( x^{\prime}\right) dx^{\prime} \] 另外在观测到$x^{\prime}$ 的条件下的$X_{n}$ 的期望值是\[ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}xf_{X_{n}}\left( x|x^{\prime}\right) dx=\int _{x^{\prime}}^{1}\frac{x}{1-x^{\prime}}dx=\frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{1-\left( x^{\prime}\right) ^{2}}{1-x^{\prime}}=\frac{1+x^{\prime}}{2} \] 因此(综合上面的计算) \begin{align*} E\left( X_{n}\right) & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}xf_{X_{n}}\left( x\right) dx\text{ }\left( \text{期望的定义}\right) \\ & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}x\left( \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f_{X_{n}}\left( x|x^{\prime}\right) f_{X_{n-1}}\left( x^{\prime}\right) dx^{\prime}\right) dx\\ & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\left( \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}xf_{X_{n}}\left( x|x^{\prime}\right) dx\right) f_{X_{n-1}}\left( x^{\prime}\right) dx^{\prime}\text{ }\left( \text{交换积分次序}\right) \\ & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{1+x^{\prime}}{2}f_{X_{n-1}}\left( x^{\prime }\right) dx^{\prime}\\ & =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}x^{\prime}f_{X_{n-1}}\left( x^{\prime}\right) dx^{\prime}\text{ }\left( \text{密度函数积分为}1\right) \\ & =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}E\left( X_{n-1}\right) \end{align*} 前面已经计算过$EX_{1}=\frac{1}{2},$ 通过递推求得\begin{align*} E\left( X_{n}\right) & =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}\left( \frac{1}{2} +\frac{1}{2}E\left( X_{n-2}\right) \right) \\ & =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{4}E\left( X_{n-2}\right) \\ & =...\\ & =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{8}+...+\frac{1}{2^{n-1}}+\frac{1}{2^{n-1} }E\left( X_{1}\right) \\ & =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{8}+...+\frac{1}{2^{n-1}}+\frac{1}{2^{n} }\\ & =1-\frac{1}{2^{n}} \end{align*}
设随机变量$X$ 取值于非负整数, 试证明\[ E\left( X\right) =\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P\left( X\geqslant n\right) =\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}P\left( X>n\right) \]
由于$X$取值于$0,1,2,...$, 按照定义 \[ E\left( X\right) =\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}nP\left( X=n\right) =\sum _{n=1}^{\infty}nP\left( X=n\right) , \] 将每一个求和项写成下列矩阵的对应行和：\[ \begin{array} [c]{ccccc} n=1: & P\left( X=1\right) & & & ...\\ n=2: & P\left( X=2\right) & P\left( X=2\right) & & ...\\ n=3: & P\left( X=3\right) & P\left( X=3\right) & P\left( X=3\right) & ...\\ ... & ... & ... & ... & ... \end{array} \] $E\left( X\right) $ 正是对这个矩阵所有非空白元素的求和, 这个求和可以用两种方式实现, 先对行求和再把各行和相加, 这正是上面定义给出的求和. 另外, 我们也可以先对各列求和, 然后再把各列总和相加, 第一列的各个概率之和为$P\left( X\geqslant1\right) ,$ 第二列的各个概率之和为$P\left( X\geqslant2\right) ,...$ 因此各列总和相加就得到 \[ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P\left( X\geqslant n\right) \] 这一求和方法当然应该和前一种求和得到相同的结果, 正是\[ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P\left( X\geqslant n\right) =\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}nP\left( X=n\right) \] 因此\[ E\left( X\right) =\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P\left( X\geqslant n\right) \] 作一个变量代换$m=n-1$, 并注意$X$ 取值于非负整数就得到\[ E\left( X\right) =\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P\left( X\geqslant n\right) =\sum_{m=0}^{\infty}P\left( X\geqslant m+1\right) =\sum_{m=0}^{\infty }P\left( X>m\right) \]
