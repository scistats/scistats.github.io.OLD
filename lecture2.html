课后习题

For any sets $A_{1},...,A_{n},$% \[ P\left( \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}A_{n}\right) \leqslant\sum_{n=1}^{\infty }P\left( A_{n}\right) \] and% \[ P\left( \bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}A_{n}\right) \geqslant1-\sum_{n=1}^{\infty }P\left( A_{n}^{c}\right) . \]
Let $B_{1}=A_{1}$, $B_{2}=A_{1}^{c}\cap A_{2}$, $B_{3}=A_{1}^{c}\cap A_{2}% ^{c}\cap A_{3}$, ... Then $B_{n}$, $n=1,2,...$, are disjoint,% \[ \sum_{n=1}^{N}B_{n}=\bigcup_{n=1}^{N}A_{n},\forall N\geqslant1. \] By probabiliy axiom% \[ \lim_{N\rightarrow\infty}\sum_{n=1}^{N}P\left( B_{n}\right) =\sum _{n=1}^{\infty}P\left( B_{n}\right) =P\left( \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}% B_{n}\right) , \] therefore% \begin{align*} P\left( \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}A_{n}\right) & =P\left( \bigcup _{n=1}^{\infty}B_{n}\right) \\ & =\lim_{N\rightarrow\infty}\sum_{n=1}^{N}P\left( B_{n}\right) \\ & =\lim_{N\rightarrow\infty}P\left( \bigcup_{n=1}^{N}A_{n}\right) \\ & \leqslant\lim_{N\rightarrow\infty}\sum_{n=1}^{N}P\left( A_{n}\right) . \end{align*}
