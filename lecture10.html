参考习题

假设$c$为常数, $f\left( x\right) $在$x=c$处连续, $X_{n}\overset{P}{\longrightarrow}c$. 那么$f\left( X_{n}\right) \overset{P}{\longrightarrow}f\left( c\right) .$
由连续性对$\forall\epsilon>0$, 存在$\delta>0$, 只要$\left\vert X_{n}-c\right\vert <\delta$, 就有$\left\vert f\left( X_{n}\right) -f\left( c\right) \right\vert <\epsilon$. 换言之\[ \left\{ \left\vert f\left( X_{n}\right) -f\left( c\right) \right\vert \geqslant\epsilon\right\} \subset\left\{ \left\vert X_{n}-c\right\vert \geqslant\delta\right\} \] 因此, 当$n\rightarrow\infty$,\[ P\left( \left\vert f\left( X_{n}\right) -f\left( c\right) \right\vert \geqslant\epsilon\right) \leqslant P\left( \left\vert X_{n}-c\right\vert \geqslant\delta\right) \rightarrow0 \] 即$f\left( X_{n}\right) \overset{P}{\longrightarrow}f\left( c\right) .$
假设$X_{1},...,X_{n}$为随机样本, 其总体方差为$\sigma^{2}.$ 证明\[ \bar{S}^{2}=\frac{1}{n\left( n-1\right) }\sum_{i< j}\left( X_{i}-X_{j}\right) ^{2}\] 是$\sigma^{2}$的无偏估计, 即$E\left( \bar{S}^{2}\right) =\sigma^{2}.$
注意到\begin{align*} \sum_{i< j}\left( X_{i}-X_{j}\right) ^{2} & =\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i,j}\left( X_{i}-X_{j}\right) ^{2}\\ & =\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i,j}\left( X_{i}^{2}+X_{j}^{2}-2X_{i}X_{j}\right) \\ & =\frac{1}{2}\sum_{i,j}\left( X_{i}^{2}+X_{j}^{2}\right) -\sum_{i,j}X_{i}X_{j}\\ & =n\sum_{i}X_{i}^{2}-\left( \sum_{i}X_{i}\right) ^{2}\end{align*}\begin{align*} \bar{S}^{2} & =\frac{1}{\left( n-1\right) }\left[ \sum_{i}X_{i}^{2}-n\left( \bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}\right] \\ & =\frac{1}{\left( n-1\right) }\sum_{i}\left( X_{i}-\bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}\end{align*} 因此$\bar{S}^{2}$是无偏估计量.
假设随机变量$X$的值域为$\left[ 0,1\right] $, 试证明$Var\left( X\right) \leqslant1/4$, 并说明等号何时成立.
由于$X$的值域为$\left[ 0,1\right] ,$ 恒成立\[ \left( X-\frac{1}{2}\right) ^{2}\leqslant\left( \frac{1}{2}\right) ^{2}\] 根据期望的极小性：\begin{align*} Var\left( X\right) & =E\left[ \left( X-E\left( X\right) \right) ^{2}\right] \leqslant E\left[ \left( X-\frac{1}{2}\right) ^{2}\right] \\ & \leqslant E\left[ \left( \frac{1}{2}\right) ^{2}\right] =\frac{1}{4}\end{align*} 这个不等式中有两个不等号, 为了第一个等号成立, 只需要$X=E\left( X\right) $, $a.e.$ 为了第二个等号成立, 即要\[ E\left[ \left( \frac{1}{2}\right) ^{2}-\left( X-\frac{1}{2}\right) ^{2}\right] =0 \] 其被积分非负, 因而必需有\[ \frac{1}{2}=\left\vert X-\frac{1}{2}\right\vert ,\text{ }a.e. \] 容易看出, 当\[ P\left( X=0\right) =P\left( X=1\right) =\frac{1}{2}\] 时, 上述两个条件同时满足, 最终就有$Var\left( X\right) =1/4.$ 当然使得$Var\left( X\right) =1/4$的随机变量并不是唯一的.
假设$X\sim Beta\left( \alpha,\beta\right) $, 试求出$X$的期望和方差.
对$k=1,2$\begin{align*} E\left( X^{k}\right) & =\frac{\Gamma\left( \alpha+\beta\right) }{\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) \Gamma\left( \beta\right) }\int_{0}^{1}x^{k}x^{\alpha}\left( 1-x\right) ^{\beta-1}dx\\ & =\frac{\Gamma\left( \alpha+\beta\right) }{\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) \Gamma\left( \beta\right) }\cdot\frac{\Gamma\left( \alpha+k\right) \Gamma\left( \beta\right) }{\Gamma\left( \alpha+\beta+k\right) }\end{align*} 从而\[ E\left( X\right) =\frac{\alpha}{\alpha+\beta},E\left( X^{2}\right) =\frac{\left( \alpha\right) \left( \alpha+1\right) }{\left( \alpha +\beta\right) \left( \alpha+\beta+1\right) }\] 因此\begin{align*} Var\left( X\right) & =E\left( X^{2}\right) -\left( E\left( X\right) \right) ^{2}\\ & =\frac{\alpha}{\alpha+\beta}\left[ \frac{\alpha+1}{\alpha+\beta+1}-\frac{\alpha}{\alpha+\beta}\right] \\ & =\frac{\alpha\beta}{\left( \alpha+\beta\right) ^{2}\left( \alpha +\beta+1\right) }\end{align*}
随机变量$X_{1},X_{2}$相互独立, $X_{i}\sim Gamma\left( \alpha_{i},\beta\right) ,$ $i=1,2$. $\alpha_{1}>0,$ $\alpha_{2}>0,$ $\beta>0$. 试运用密度卷积公式直接证明$X_{1}+X_{2}\sim Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2},\beta\right) $.
由于$X_{1},X_{2}$相互独立, 根据密度卷积公式, $X_{1}+X_{2}$的密度函数为\begin{align*} f\left( z\right) & =\int_{0}^{\infty}f_{X_{1}}\left( z-x\right) f_{X_{2}}\left( x\right) dx\\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}\right) \Gamma\left( \alpha_{2}\right) }\int_{0}^{z}\left[ \left( z-x\right) ^{\alpha_{1}-1}e^{-\beta\left( z-x\right) }\right] \left[ x^{\alpha_{2}-1}e^{-\beta x}\right] dx\\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}\right) \Gamma\left( \alpha_{2}\right) }\cdot e^{-\beta z}\cdot\int_{0}^{z}\left( z-x\right) ^{\alpha_{1}-1}x^{\alpha_{2}-1}dx\\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}\right) \Gamma\left( \alpha_{2}\right) }\cdot z^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}-1}e^{-\beta z}\cdot\int_{0}^{1}\left( 1-y\right) ^{\alpha_{1}-1}y^{\alpha_{2}-1}dy\text{ \ }\left( yz=x\right) \\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}\right) \Gamma\left( \alpha_{2}\right) }\cdot z^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}-1}e^{-\beta z}\cdot\frac{\Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}\right) \Gamma\left( \alpha_{2}\right) }{\Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}\right) }\\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}+\alpha _{2}\right) }\cdot z^{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}-1}e^{-\beta z}\end{align*} 因此$X_{1}+X_{2}\sim Gamma\left( \alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2},\beta\right) $.
