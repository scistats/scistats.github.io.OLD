课后习题

假设随机变量$X_{1},...,X_{n}$为随机样本, 总体$X\sim N\left( \mu,\sigma^{2}\right) .$ 令\[ S^{2}=\frac{1}{n-1}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}, \] 试求出 $Var\left( S^{2}\right) $.
由于\[ \frac{1}{\sigma^{2}}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}\sim\chi^{2}\left( n-1\right) =Gamma\left( \frac{n-1}{2},\frac{1}{2}\right) \] 因此\begin{align*} Var\left( S^{2}\right) & =Var\left( \frac{\sigma^{2}}{n-1}\cdot\frac {1}{\sigma^{2}}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}\right) \\ & =\left( \frac{\sigma^{2}}{n-1}\right) ^{2}\cdot2\left( n-1\right) =\frac{2\sigma^{4}}{n-1}\end{align*}
假设随机变量$X_{1},...,X_{n}$为随机样本, 总体$X\sim N\left( \mu,\sigma^{2}\right) $. (1)确定只与$n$有关的常数$c_{1}$使得$c_{1}\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\mu\right) ^{2}}$为$\sigma$的无偏估计量; (2)确定只与$n$有关的常数$c_{2}$使得$c_{2}\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}}$为$\sigma $的无偏估计量.
(1) $\frac{1}{\sigma^{2}}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\mu\right) ^{2}\sim \chi^{2}\left( n\right) ,$ 因此\begin{align*} E\left( \sqrt{\frac{1}{\sigma^{2}}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\mu\right) ^{2}}\right) & =\frac{1}{2^{\frac{n}{2}}\Gamma\left( \frac{n}{2}\right) }\int_{0}^{\infty}x^{\frac{1}{2}}\cdot x^{\frac{n}{2}-1}e^{-\frac{1}{2}x}dx\\ & =\frac{1}{2^{\frac{n}{2}}\Gamma\left( \frac{n}{2}\right) }\cdot 2^{\frac{n+1}{2}}\Gamma\left( \frac{n+1}{2}\right) \\ & =\frac{2^{\frac{1}{2}}\Gamma\left( \frac{n+1}{2}\right) }{\Gamma\left( \frac{n}{2}\right) }\end{align*} 由此\[ c_{1}=\frac{\Gamma\left( \frac{n}{2}\right) }{2^{\frac{1}{2}}\Gamma\left( \frac{n+1}{2}\right) }\] (2) $\frac{1}{\sigma^{2}}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}\sim\chi^{2}\left( n-1\right) ,$ 类似于(1)的计算可得到\[ c_{2}=\frac{\Gamma\left( \frac{n-1}{2}\right) }{2^{\frac{1}{2}}\Gamma\left( \frac{n}{2}\right) }\]
假设随机变量$X_{1},...,X_{n}$为随机样本, 总体$X$服从参数$n$和$p$的二项分布. 试求出$\left( n,p\right) $的矩估计量.
建立方程组\[ \frac{1}{n}{\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{n}} X_{i}=np \]\[ \frac{1}{n}{\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{n}} X_{i}^{2}=np\left( 1-p\right) +n^{2}p^{2}\] 解之得到$\left( n,p\right) $的矩估计量为\[ \tilde{n}=\frac{\bar{X}_{n}^{2}}{\bar{X}_{n}-\left( 1/n\right) \sum _{i=1}^{n}\left( X_{i}-\bar{X}_{n}\right) ^{2}},\text{ }\tilde{p}=\frac {\bar{X}_{n}}{\tilde{n}}\]
假设随机变量$X_{1},...,X_{n}$为随机样本, 总体分布为$\left[ 0,\theta\right] $上的均匀分布, $\theta>0.$ 其密度函数为\[ f\left( x\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc}\frac{1}{\theta}, & 0< x< \theta;\\ 0, & \text{其他.}\end{array} \right. . \] 那么不存在关于$\theta$的最大似然估计. 判断该结论是否正确, 并说明理由.
记$x=\left( x_{1},...,x_{n}\right) ^{T}.$ 似然函数为\[ L\left( \theta|x\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc}\frac{1}{\theta^{n}}, & 0< x_{i}< \theta,i=1,...,n;\\ 0, & \text{其他.}\end{array} \right. \] 从中可看到$\theta>\max\left\{ x_{1},...,x_{n}\right\} .$ $L\left( \theta|x\right) $随着$\theta $减少而增加, 但$\theta$可以无限接近$\max\left\{ x_{1},...,x_{n}\right\} $却不能达到, 因此$\theta$没有最大似然估计.
假设随机变量$X_{1},...,X_{n}$为随机样本, 总体分布为$\left[ \theta,\theta+1\right] $上的均匀分布, $\theta\in\mathbb{R}.$ 其密度函数为\[ f\left( x\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc}1, & \theta\leqslant x\leqslant\theta+1;\\ 0, & \text{其他.}\end{array} \right. . \] 是说明关于$\theta$的最大似然估计不是唯一的.
记$x=\left( x_{1},...,x_{n}\right) ^{T}.$ 似然函数为\[ L\left( \theta|x\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc}1, & \theta\leqslant x_{i}\leqslant\theta+1,i=1,...,n;\\ 0, & \text{其他.}\end{array} \right. \] 从中可看到$\theta\in\left[ \max\left\{ x_{1},...,x_{n}\right\} -1,\min\left\{ x_{1},...,x_{n}\right\} \right] .$ 而$L\left( \theta|x\right) $的最大值是$1$, 只要$\theta\in\left[ \max\left\{ x_{1},...,x_{n}\right\} -1,\min\left\{ x_{1},...,x_{n}\right\} \right] $. 因此$\theta$的最大似然估计不是唯一的.
