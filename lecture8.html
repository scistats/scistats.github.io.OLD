随机变量$X_{1},...,X_{n}$相互独立, $X_{k}$服从参数为$\beta$的指数分布, 那么$Y_{1}=\min\left\{ X_{1},\cdot\cdot\cdot,X_{n}\right\} $服从参数为$n\beta$的指数分布$.$
由独立性\begin{align*} P\left( Y_{1}>t\right) & =P\left( \min\left\{ X_{1},\cdot\cdot \cdot,X_{n}\right\} >t\right) \\ & =\prod_{k=1}^{n}P\left( X_{k}>t\right) \\ & =\prod_{k=1}^{n}e^{-\beta t}=e^{-n\beta t}\end{align*} 因此$Y_{1}$服从参数为$n\beta$的指数分布.
试计算Gamma分布的期望, 方差, 与生成函数.
假设$X$服从参数为$\alpha>0$, $\beta>0$的Gamma分布, 密度函数为\[ f\left( x|\alpha,\beta\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc}% \frac{\beta^{\alpha}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\beta x}, & x>0\\ 0, & x\leqslant0 \end{array} \right. \] 其中\[ \Gamma\left( \alpha\right) =\int_{0}^{\infty}x^{\alpha-1}e^{-x}dx \] 由分部积分容易验证$\alpha >1$时$\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) =\left( \alpha-1\right) \Gamma\left( \alpha-1\right) $. (1) $\forall k=1,2,...$, \begin{align*} E\left( X^{k}\right) & =\int_{0}^{\infty}x^{k}\frac{\beta^{\alpha}}% {\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\beta x}dx\\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }\cdot\frac{1}% {\beta^{k+\alpha}}\int_{0}^{\infty}x^{k+\alpha-1}e^{-x}dx\\ & =\frac{\Gamma\left( \alpha+k\right) }{\beta^{k}\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }. \end{align*} 因此$E\left( X\right) =\frac{\Gamma\left( \alpha+1\right) }{\beta\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }=\frac{\alpha}{\beta}.$ (2) \begin{align*} Var\left( X\right) & =E\left( X^{2}\right) -\left( E\left( X\right) \right) ^{2}\\ & =\frac{\Gamma\left( \alpha+2\right) }{\beta^{2}\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }-\left( \frac{\alpha}{\beta}\right) ^{2}\\ & =\frac{\left( \alpha+1\right) \alpha}{\beta^{2}}-\left( \frac{\alpha }{\beta}\right) ^{2}\\ & =\frac{\alpha}{\beta^{2}}% \end{align*} (3) 当$\beta-t>0$时$E\left( e^{tX}\right) $存在, 且有\begin{align*} \psi\left( t\right) & =E\left( e^{tX}\right) =\int_{0}^{\infty}% e^{tx}\frac{\beta^{\alpha}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }x^{\alpha -1}e^{-\beta x}dx\\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }\int_{0}^{\infty }x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\left( \beta-t\right) x}dx\\ & =\frac{\beta^{\alpha}}{\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }\cdot\frac {\Gamma\left( \alpha\right) }{\left( \beta-t\right) ^{\alpha}}=\left( \frac{\beta}{\beta-t}\right) ^{\alpha}% \end{align*}
随机变量$X_{1},X_{2}$相互独立, $X_{j}$服从参数为$\lambda_{j}$ $\left( j=1,2\right) $的Poisson分布, 试通过密度卷积公式证明$X_{1}+X_{2}$服从参数为$\lambda_{1}+\lambda_{2}$的Poisson分布$.$
由于$X_{1},X_{2}$相互独立, 根据密度卷积公式, $Y=X_{1}+X_{2}$的密度为, $\forall k=0,1,...$\begin{align*} f\left( k\right) & =\sum_{0\leqslant i\leqslant k}f_{X_{1}}\left( k-i\right) f_{X_{2}}\left( i\right) \\ & =\sum_{0\leqslant i\leqslant k}e^{-\lambda_{1}}\frac{\lambda_{1}^{\left( k-i\right) }}{\left( k-i\right) !}\cdot e^{-\lambda_{2}}\frac{\lambda _{2}^{i}}{i!}\\ & =e^{-\left( \lambda_{1}+\lambda_{2}\right) }\cdot\frac{1}{k!}\cdot \sum_{0\leqslant i\leqslant k}\frac{k!}{\left( k-i\right) !i!}\lambda _{1}^{\left( k-i\right) }\lambda_{2}^{i}\\ & =e^{-\left( \lambda_{1}+\lambda_{2}\right) }\frac{\left( \lambda _{1}+\lambda_{2}\right) ^{k}}{k!}\end{align*}
随机变量$X_{1},X_{2}$相互独立, $X_{k}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( \mu_{k},\sigma_{k}^{2}\right) $, $k=1,2$. 试通过密度卷积公式证明\[ Y=X_{1}+X_{2}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( \mu_{1}+\mu_{2},\sigma_{2}^{2}+\sigma _{2}^{2}\right) . \]
由于$X_{1},X_{2}$相互独立, 根据密度卷积公式, $Y=X_{1}+X_{2}$的密度为, $\forall x$\begin{align*} f\left( x\right) & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f_{X_{1}}\left( x-z\right) f_{X_{2}}\left( z\right) dz\\ & ^{2}\] 它可以写成如下形式,\[ h\left( z\right) =\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left( z+\ast\right) ^{2}+\min_{z}h\left( z\right) \] 为计算积分, 只需求出$\min_{z}h\left( z\right) $, 令$h\left( \bar{z}\right) =\min_{z}h\left( z\right) $, 由极值必要条件$h^{\prime}\left( \bar{z}\right) =0$得\[ \frac{1}{\sigma_{1}}\left( \frac{\bar{z}+\mu_{1}-x}{\sigma_{1}}\right) +\frac{1}{\sigma_{2}}\left( \frac{\bar{z}-\mu_{2}}{\sigma_{2}}\right) =0 \] 因此\[ \left( \frac{x-\bar{z}-\mu_{1}}{\sigma_{1}}\right) ^{2}=\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}}{\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left( \frac{\bar{z}-\mu_{2}}{\sigma_{2}}\right) ^{2}\] 同时也解得\[ \bar{z}=\frac{1}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left[ \left( x-\mu _{1}\right) \sigma_{2}^{2}+\mu_{2}\sigma_{1}^{2}\right] \] 容易求得\[ \frac{\bar{z}-\mu_{2}}{\sigma_{2}}=\frac{\sigma_{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma _{2}^{2}}\left( x-\mu_{1}-\mu_{2}\right) \] 将上面的计算都带入$h\left( z\right) $便得到\begin{align*} h\left( \bar{z}\right) & =\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}}{\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left( \frac{\bar{z}-\mu_{2}}{\sigma_{2}}\right) ^{2}+\left( \frac{\bar{z}-\mu_{2}}{\sigma_{2}}\right) ^{2}\\ & =\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left( \frac{\bar {z}-\mu_{2}}{\sigma_{2}}\right) ^{2}\\ & =\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left[ \frac {\sigma_{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left( x-\mu_{1}-\mu_{2}\right) \right] ^{2}\\ & =\frac{\left( x-\mu_{1}-\mu_{2}\right) ^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma _{2}^{2}}\end{align*} 回到最初的积分式子\begin{align*} f\left( x\right) & =\frac{1}{2\pi\sigma_{1}\sigma_{2}}\int_{-\infty }^{\infty}\exp\left\{ -\frac{1}{2}\left[ \frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left( z+\ast\right) ^{2}+\frac{\left( x-\mu_{1}-\mu_{2}\right) ^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\right] \right\} dz\\ & =\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\frac{\left( x-\mu_{1}-\mu_{2}\right) ^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\right] \cdot\frac{1}{2\pi\sigma_{1}\sigma_{2}}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}\sigma_{2}^{2}}\left( z+\ast\right) ^{2}\right] dz\\ & =\frac{1}{\left( 2\pi\right) ^{1/2}\left( \sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}\right) ^{1/2}}\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\frac{\left( x-\mu_{1}-\mu _{2}\right) ^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\right] \frac{1}{\left( 2\pi\right) ^{1/2}}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\left( y+\ast\right) ^{2}\right] dy\\ & =\frac{1}{\left( 2\pi\right) ^{1/2}\left( \sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}\right) ^{1/2}}\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\frac{\left( x-\mu_{1}-\mu _{2}\right) ^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\right] \end{align*} 其中第二至第三个等号用了代换$\left( \frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}\sigma_{2}^{2}}\right) ^{1/2}z=y$. 可见$f\left( x\right) $正是$\mathcal{N}\left( \mu_{1}+\mu_{2},\sigma_{2}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}\right) $的密度.
试写出标准正态分布对应于$0.5,0.25,0.75$的分位值.
注意正态分布密度连续点点大于$0$, 因此其分布函数$y=\Phi\left( x\right) $严格递增, 从而存在逆函数, 正太分布的分位函数正是其逆函数$\Phi ^{-1}\left( y\right) $. 由对称性可得到$\Phi^{-1}\left( 0.5\right) =0$, $\Phi^{-1}\left( 0.25\right) =-\Phi^{-1}\left( 0.75\right) $. $\Phi^{-1}\left( 0.75\right) $可以通过查询标准正太分布表或是由统计软件求得, $\Phi^{-1}\left( 0.75\right) =0.6745,$ 从而$\Phi^{-1}\left( 0.25\right) =-0.6745.$
假设$X\sim\mathcal{N}\left( \mu,2\right) $. 现抽取$25$个$X$的观测值, 其抽样平均值落入$\left( \mu-1,\mu+1\right) $的概率为多少?
用$\bar{X}_{n}$表示样本$\left\{ X_{i}\right\} _{i=1}^{n}$的均值, $n=25$. 由条件$E\left( \bar{X}_{n}\right) =\mu$, $Var\left( \bar{X}_{n}\right) =2/n=2/25.$ 因此$\left( \bar{X}_{n}-\mu\right) /\left( 2/25\right) ^{1/2}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 0,1\right) $. 从而\[ P\left( \left\vert \bar{X}_{n}-\mu\right\vert <1\right) =2\Phi\left( \left( 25/2\right) ^{1/2}\right) -1 \]
用$X_{1},X_{2},X_{3}$表示三根内存条的使用寿命, 假设$X_{k}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 300\text{小时},10\text{小时}\right) $, $k=1,2,3$. 试求出至少有一根内存条可以使用到$290$小时的概率.
令$A_{k}=\left\{ X_{k}<290\right\} $. 要求的概率为\begin{align*} P\left( A_{1}^{c}\cup A_{2}^{c}\cup A_{3}^{c}\right) & =1-P\left( A_{1}\cap A_{2}\cap A_{3}\right) \\ & =1-P\left( A_{1}\right) P\left( A_{2}\right) P\left( A_{3}\right) \text{ (独立性)}\end{align*} 由题\begin{align*} P\left( A_{k}\right) & =P\left( \frac{X_{k}-300}{\sqrt{10}}<\frac{290-300}{\sqrt{10}}\right) \\ & =\Phi\left( -\sqrt{10}\right) \end{align*} 因此所求概率为$1-\left( \Phi\left( -\sqrt{10}\right) \right) ^{3}$
