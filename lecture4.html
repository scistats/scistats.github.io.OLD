课后习题

随机变量$X,Y$ 具有联合密度函数\[ f\left( x,y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} cx^{2}y^{2}, & x^{2}+y^{2}\leqslant1\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] (1)确定关于$X$和$Y$ 的边际密度函数. (2) 判断$X$与$Y$的独立性
(1)注意$x,y$的对称性, 只需要求$f_{X}\left( x\right) $或$f_{X}\left( y\right) $. \[ f_{X}\left( x\right) =\int_{-\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}^{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}cx^{2} y^{2}dy=\frac{2c}{3}x^{2}\left( 1-x^{2}\right) ^{3/2},-1\leqslant x\leqslant1 \] (2)由(1)的计算可知$X$与$Y$不独立.
随机变量$X,Y$ 具有联合密度函数\[ f\left( x,y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} \frac{3}{16}\left( 4-2x-y\right) , & x>0,y>0\text{并且}2x+y<4\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] (1)确定给定$X$时$Y$ 的条件密度函数. (2) 确定$P\left( Y\geqslant2|X=0.5\right) .$
先求出\begin{align*} f_{X}\left( x\right) & =\int_{0}^{4-2x}\frac{3}{16}\left( 4-2x-y\right) dy=\frac{3}{16}\left[ \left( 4-2x\right) ^{2}-\frac{1}{2}\left( 4-2x\right) ^{2}\right] \\ & =\frac{3}{32}\left( 4-2x\right) ^{2},0< x< 2 \end{align*} 因此\[ f_{Y}\left( y|x\right) =\frac{f\left( x,y\right) }{f_{X}\left( x\right) }=\frac{\left( 4-2x-y\right) }{2\left( 2-x\right) ^{2}},0< x< 2,y< 4-2x. \] 当$X=0.5,$ $y< 4-2x=3,$ \[ P\left( Y\geqslant2|X=0.5\right) =\int_{2}^{3}f_{Y}\left( y|\frac{1} {2}\right) =\int_{2}^{3}\frac{2}{9}\left( 3-y\right) dy=\frac{1}{9} \]
随机变量$X$与$Y$ 相互独立, 其密度函数分别为$f_{X}\left( x\right) ,f_{X}\left( y\right) .$ 试求概率$P\left( X< Y\right) $.
由条件概率的性质以及独立性\begin{align*} P\left( X< Y\right) & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}P\left( x< Y|X=x\right) f_{X}\left( x\right) dx\\ & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}P\left( x< Y\right) f_{X}\left( x\right) dx\\ & =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\left( \int_{x}^{\infty}f_{Y}\left( y\right) dy\right) f_{X}\left( x\right) dx \end{align*}
令$Y$ 为客服中心每小时接到电话的数量, $X$ 为一个两小时的时段内接到的电话数量. 假设$Y$的分布密度为, \[ f_{Y}\left( y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} e^{-y}, & y>0\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] 给定$Y=y$时$X$ 的条件分布为, \[ f_{X}\left( x|y\right) =\left\{ \begin{array} [c]{cc} \frac{\left( 2y\right) ^{x}}{x!}e^{-2y}, & x=0,1,2....\\ 0, & \text{其他} \end{array} \right. \] (1)确定关于$X$ 的边际分布. (2)确定给定$X=0$时$Y$ 的条件密度函数 (3)确定给定$X=1$时$Y$ 的条件密度函数
(1)先写出联合密度\[ f\left( x,y\right) =f_{X}\left( x|y\right) f_{Y}\left( y\right) =\frac{\left( 2y\right) ^{x}}{x!}e^{-3y},x=0,1,2....,y>0 \] 因此\[ f_{X}\left( x\right) =\int_{0}^{\infty}f\left( x,y\right) dy=\frac{1} {x!}\int_{0}^{\infty}\left( 2y\right) ^{x}e^{-3y}dy \] 其中若令\[ I_{x}=\int_{0}^{\infty}\left( 2y\right) ^{x}e^{-3y}dy \] 那么\begin{align*} I_{x} & =-\frac{1}{3}\int_{0}^{\infty}\left( 2y\right) ^{x}d\left( e^{-3y}\right) \\ & =\frac{2}{3}x\int_{0}^{\infty}\left( 2y\right) ^{x-1}e^{-3y}dy=\frac{2} {3}xI_{x-1} \end{align*} 推得\[ I_{x}=\left( \frac{2}{3}\right) ^{x}x!I_{0}=\frac{1}{3}\left( \frac{2} {3}\right) ^{x}x! \] 因此\[ f_{X}\left( x\right) =\frac{1}{x!}I_{x}=\frac{1}{3}\left( \frac{2} {3}\right) ^{x},x=0,1,2.... \] (2) \[ f_{Y}\left( y|0\right) =\left. \frac{f\left( x,y\right) }{f_{X}\left( x\right) }\right\vert _{x=0}=3e^{-3y},y>0. \] (3) \[ f_{Y}\left( y|1\right) =\left. \frac{f\left( x,y\right) }{f_{X}\left( x\right) }\right\vert _{x=1}=9ye^{-3y},y>0. \]
