假设$\left( X_{1},X_{2}\right) \sim\mathcal{N}\left( 0;I\right) $, 试求$P\left( -\sqrt{2}< X_{1}+X_{2}<2\sqrt {2}\right) .$
由条件$X_{1}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 0,1\right) ,$ $X_{2}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 0,1\right) ,$ 且相互独立. 因此$X_{1}+X_{2}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 0,1+1\right) =\mathcal{N}\left( 0,2\right) $, 从而$\left( X_{1}+X_{2}\right) /\sqrt{2}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 0,1\right) ,$\begin{align*} & P\left( -\sqrt{2}< X_{1}+X_{2}<2\sqrt{2}\right) \\ & =P\left( -1<\frac{X_{1}+X_{2}}{\sqrt{2}}<2\right) \\ & =\Phi\left( 2\right) -\Phi\left( -1\right) =\Phi\left( 2\right) -\left[ 1-\Phi\left( 1\right) \right] \end{align*} 其中$\Phi\left( x\right) $是标准正太分布的分布函数.
假设$\left( X_{1},X_{2}\right) \sim\mathcal{N}\left( 0;I\right) $, 试求$X_{1}/X_{2}$的分布$.$
先求出$X_{1}/X_{2}$与$X_{2}$的联合分布. 令$Y_{1}=X_{1}/X_{2}$, $Y_{2}=X_{2}$, 那么$X_{1}=Y_{1}Y_{2}$, $X_{2}=Y_{2}$, \[ J=\left\vert \begin{array} [c]{cc}\frac{\partial x_{1}}{\partial y_{1}} & \frac{\partial x_{1}}{\partial y_{2}}\\ \frac{\partial x_{2}}{\partial y_{1}} & \frac{\partial x_{2}}{\partial y_{2}}\end{array} \right\vert =\left\vert \begin{array} [c]{cc}y_{2} & y_{1}\\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right\vert =y_{2}\] 由条件$\left( X_{1},X_{2}\right) $的联合密度为\[ f\left( x_{1},x_{2}\right) =\frac{1}{2\pi}\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\left( x_{1}^{2}+x_{1}^{2}\right) \right] \] 因此由密度变换公式, $\left( Y_{1},Y_{2}\right) $的联合密度为\begin{align*} g\left( y_{1},y_{2}\right) & =f\left( y_{1}y_{2},y_{2}\right) \cdot\left\vert y_{2}\right\vert \\ & =\frac{1}{2\pi}\left\vert y_{2}\right\vert \exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\left( y_{1}^{2}y_{2}^{2}+y_{2}^{2}\right) \right] \end{align*} 从而$X_{1}/X_{2}=Y_{1}$的密度即为关于$Y_{1}$的边际密度, $\forall y_{1}\in\mathbb{R}$\begin{align*} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}g\left( y_{1},y_{2}\right) dy_{2} & =\frac{1}{\pi }\int_{0}^{\infty}y_{2}\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\left( y_{1}^{2}y_{2}^{2}+y_{2}^{2}\right) \right] dy_{2}\\ & =\frac{1}{\pi\left( 1+y_{1}^{2}\right) }. \end{align*}
假设$\log X\sim\mathcal{N}\left( \mu;\sigma^{2}\right) $, 试求$1/X$的分布$.$
$\Phi\left( x\right) $是标准正太分布的分布函数. $1/X$的分布为\begin{align*} F_{1/X}\left( z\right) & =P\left( 1/X\leqslant z\right) \\ & =P\left( -\log X\leqslant\log z\right) \\ & =P\left( \log X\geqslant-\log z\right) \\ & =P\left( \frac{\log X-\mu}{\sigma}\geqslant\frac{-\log z-\mu}{\sigma }\right) \\ & =1-\Phi\left( \frac{-\log z-\mu}{\sigma}\right) \end{align*} 因此$1/X$的密度为\[ \frac{1}{\sigma z}\cdot\Phi^{\prime}\left( \frac{-\log z-\mu}{\sigma}\right) =\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma}\frac{1}{z}\exp\left[ -\frac{1}{2}\left( \frac{-\log z-\mu}{\sigma}\right) ^{2}\right] \]
假设$\log X\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 3;1.44\right) $,试求$P\left( X\leqslant6.05\right) .$
令$\Phi\left( x\right) $为标准正太分布的分布函数.\[ P\left( X\leqslant6.05\right) =P\left( \frac{\log X-3}{\sqrt{1.44}}\leqslant\frac{\log\left( 6.05\right) -3}{\sqrt{1.44}}\right) =\Phi\left( -1\right) \]
假设$\left( X_{1},X_{2}\right) \sim\mathcal{N}\left( \mu _{1},\mu_{2};\sigma_{1}^{2},\sigma_{2}^{2},\rho\right) $, 试证明$X_{1}+X_{2}$与$X_{1}-X_{2}$相互独立的充分必要条件是$\sigma _{1}=\sigma_{2}.$
$\left( X_{1}+X_{2},X_{1}-X_{2}\right) $是由$\left( X_{1},X_{2}\right) $经过线性变换得到的, 由正太分布的性质, $\left( X_{1}+X_{2},X_{1}-X_{2}\right) $具有二元正态分布. 因此其分量的独立性等价于不相关性, 即当且仅当\[ cov\left( X_{1}+X_{2},X_{1}-X_{2}\right) =0 \] 而\begin{align*} & cov\left( X_{1}+X_{2},X_{1}-X_{2}\right) \\ & =cov\left( X_{1},X_{1}\right) -cov\left( X_{1},X_{2}\right) +cov\left( X_{2},X_{1}\right) -cov\left( X_{2},X_{2}\right) \\ & =\sigma_{1}^{2}-\sigma_{2}^{2}\end{align*} 因此独立当且仅当$\sigma _{1}=\sigma_{2}$.
假设$X_{k}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( \mu_{k};\sigma_{k}^{2}\right) ,$ $k=1,2$, 相互独立. 试写出$X_{1}+X_{2}$与$X_{1}-X_{2}$的相关系数与联合分布.
$\left( X_{1}+X_{2},X_{1}-X_{2}\right) $由$\left( X_{1},X_{2}\right) $经过线性变换得到, 因而具有二元正态分布, 其分布由期望和协方差矩阵确定. 由题设\[ E\left( X_{1}\pm X_{2}\right) =\mu_{1}\pm\mu_{2},Var\left( X_{1}\pm X_{2}\right) =\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}\] 相关系数为\[ \rho=\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}-\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sqrt{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\sqrt{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}}=\frac{\sigma_{1}^{2}-\sigma_{2}^{2}}{\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}}\] 因此$\left( X_{1}+X_{2},X_{1}-X_{2}\right) \sim\mathcal{N}\left( \mu;C\right) ,$ 其中\[ \mu=\left( \mu_{1},\mu_{2}\right) ^{T},C=\left( \begin{array} [c]{cc}\sigma_{1}^{2} & \rho\sigma_{1}\sigma_{2}\\ \rho\sigma_{1}\sigma_{2} & \sigma_{2}^{2}\end{array} \right) \]
对某一分布进行随机抽样, 试问样本数目$n$应为多大才能使得样本平均$\bar {X}_{n}$与真实的分布期望值$\mu $误差不超过$2$倍标准差的概率在$0.99$以上?
根据Chebyshev不等式,\[ P\left( \left\vert \bar{X}_{n}-\mu\right\vert \leqslant2\sigma\right) \geqslant1-\frac{Var\left( \bar{X}_{n}\right) }{4\sigma^{2}}=1-\frac{1}{4n}\] 为使$P\left( \left\vert \bar{X}_{n}-\mu\right\vert \leqslant 2\sigma\right) \geqslant0.99,$ 只要$1-\frac{1}{4n}\geqslant 0.99,n\geqslant25.$
随机地从$\left\{ 0,...,9\right\} $中选取$16$个数字(可以重复), 其平均值介于$4$和$6$之间的概率为多少?
令$X_{i}$表示第$i$个随机选取的数字, 那么$X_{1},...,X_{16}$独立同分布. 且\[ E\left( X_{i}\right) =\frac{1}{10}\left( 0+1+...+9\right) =4.5 \]\[ E\left( X_{i}^{2}\right) =\frac{1}{10}\left( 0^{2}+1^{2}+...+9^{2}\right) =\left. \frac{n\left( n+1\right) \left( 2n+1\right) }{6}\right\vert _{n=9}=28.5 \] $Var\left( X\right) =28.5-\left( 4.5\right) ^{2}=8.25$. 由中心极限定理, 近似地$\bar{X}_{16}\sim\mathcal{N}\left( 4.5;8.25/16\right) $, 因此\begin{align*} P\left( 4\leqslant\bar{X}_{16}\leqslant6\right) & =P\left( \frac {4-4.5}{\sqrt{8.25/16}}\leqslant\frac{\bar{X}_{16}-4.5}{\sqrt{8.25/16}}\leqslant\frac{6-4.5}{\sqrt{8.25/16}}\right) \\ & \approx P\left( -0.70\leqslant\frac{\bar{X}_{16}-4.5}{\sqrt{8.25/16}}\leqslant2.089\right) \\ & \approx\Phi\left( 2.089\right) -\Phi\left( -0.70\right) \end{align*} 其中$\Phi\left( x\right) $是标准正太分布的分布函数.
现有$n$张标有不同图案的卡片, 对应于每张卡片都有一个信封, 其封面上有一个与对应卡片相同的图案. 现将$n$张卡片随机地放入$n$个信封. 每个信封只能放一张卡片. 令$S_{n}$表示与信封相匹配的卡片的数目. 试证明$\frac{S_{n}-E\left( S_{n}\right) }{n}\overset{P}{\longrightarrow}0$ ($n\rightarrow\infty$)$.$
令$X_{i}=1$表示第$i$个卡片放入了与其对应的信封, $X_{i}=0$表示第$i$个卡片没有放入与其对应的信封. 容易求得\[ E\left( X_{i}\right) =\frac{1}{n},Var\left( X_{i}\right) =E\left( X_{i}^{2}\right) -\frac{1}{n^{2}}=\frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{n^{2}}, \]\[ cov\left( X_{i},X_{j}\right) =E\left( X_{i}X_{j}\right) -E\left( X_{i}\right) E\left( X_{j}\right) =\frac{1}{n\left( n-1\right) }-\frac {1}{n^{2}}\] 从而\begin{align*} Var\left( S_{n}\right) & =\sum_{i=1}^{n}Var\left( X_{i}\right) +2\sum_{i< j}^{n}cov\left( X_{i},X_{j}\right) \\ & =n\left( \frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{n^{2}}\right) +n\left( n-1\right) \left( \frac{1}{n\left( n-1\right) }-\frac{1}{n^{2}}\right) \\ & =1 \end{align*} 因此根据Chebyshev不等式, $\forall\epsilon>0,$\[ P\left( \left\vert \frac{S_{n}-E\left( S_{n}\right) }{n}\right\vert \geqslant\epsilon\right) \leqslant\frac{Var\left( S_{n}\right) }{\epsilon^{2}n^{2}}=\frac{1}{\epsilon^{2}n^{2}}\rightarrow0 \] 即$\frac{S_{n}-E\left( S_{n}\right) }{n}\overset{P}{\longrightarrow}0$.
